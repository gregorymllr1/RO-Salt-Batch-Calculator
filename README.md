# RO Feed Batch Calculator 💧

Welcome to the **RO Feed Batch Calculator**, a lightweight, web-based utility designed for the rapid and precise preparation of synthetic feed water solutions. 

Whether you are scaling up a pilot system or characterizing membrane performance and transport physics at the bench scale, this tool eliminates the guesswork from feed preparation. By inputting your target parameters, the calculator instantly provides the required salt masses, resulting osmotic pressure, and estimated batch costs.

## ✨ Features

* **Precision Mass Calculation:** Input your desired target concentration (in ppm or mg/L) for a customized list of salts, and the calculator determines the exact mass required for your specific batch volume.
* **Osmotic Pressure Estimation:** Automatically calculates the theoretical osmotic pressure (π) of the mixed feed solution based on the input salt concentrations and water temperature, providing critical data for evaluating driving force and membrane permeability.
* **Temperature Compensation:** Adjusts thermodynamic calculations based on the user-defined temperature of the feed water.
* **Cost Analysis:** Integrates unit costs for each salt to provide an immediate total cost estimate for the prepared batch, aiding in project budgeting and pilot-scale forecasting.
